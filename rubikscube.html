🎮 Controls

Letter Keys:

R / Shift+R - Right face

L / Shift+L - Left face

U / Shift+U - Top face

D / Shift+D - Bottom face

F / Shift+F - Front face

B / Shift+B - Back face

M / Shift+M - Middle slice

E / Shift+E - Equator slice

View:

Arrow Keys - Rotate view

Mouse Drag - Rotate view

S - Scramble cube

📐 Cube Notation

U Top
L Left
F Front
R Right
B Back
D Bottom

Hold Shift for counter-clockwise

Drag to rotate view • Letter keys to rotate faces (Shift=reverse)
Moves: 0
